Who I am

Software Developer based out of Sarasota, FL

I began programming about halfway through 2020. I went from not knowing the significance of the phrase “Hello, World!” to knowing how to plan, program, and deploy front and back end applications. I used courses and tutorials to develop an entire new skill set while working full-time. I am very organized and detail oriented, I have a passion for problem solving and developing new skills. I am a real team player and I’m looking forward to collaborating with like-minded individuals to develop important and useful applications. All of the applications I've developed so far have utilized some combination of the following technologies; ES6 JavaScript, React.JS, Node.JS, RESTful APIs, Express, HTML5, CSS3, and PostgrSQL.

When I’m not programming, I enjoy listening to and playing music, as I am a multi-instrumentalist. I love cooking and enjoying delicious vegan food. My favorite way to spend my time when I’m not programming is in nature. I love hiking and enjoying the natural beauty of the world.
